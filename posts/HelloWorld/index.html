Hello World - Be Welcome

Jan 6 2021-01-06T01:50:00-03:00 by Eduardo Adame
2 min

A new hello world.

Hello, my dear visitor, be welcome. Today I’m starting a brand new project, my new blog. So, I think I have to present myself to you all. I am Eduardo Adame, I am 17 years old, and I love Math and Tech stuff. My main idea here is to share what I know about things I like - usually math-related.

Alright, how will the blog work out?

I haven’t said this right before, but I am Brazilian. So, my mother language is Portuguese, and I am supposed to write things in this language. Why am I writing in English? First: I want to lend my “knowledge” to most people I can. Second: I want to have a routine of writing in this language, even if I commit some mistakes.

Blog features.

This blog is powered by Jekyll, a static website generator made with Ruby - a programming language. Because of this, I use Markdown to write the articles, and so I can use code blocks and math expressions.

For instance:

We can say that the derivative global definition can be defined by

\[\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \lim_{h \to 0} \dfrac{f(x+h)- f(x)}{h}\]

Or solve a short problem:

\[\begin{aligned} \lim_{x \to 2} \dfrac{x^2 - 4}{x - 2} &= \lim_{x \to 2} \dfrac{(x+2)(x-2)}{x-2} \\\\ &= \lim_{x \to 2} x+2\\\\ &= 4 \end{aligned}\]
